Product:        Dwarf Mayhem: Into the Depths
Team:           Team EggCatRed

In Dwarf Mayhem, you play as a dwarf whose main objective is to mine your 
way to the core of the earth. The resources that you have mined can be 
traded in for various upgrades in the shop on the surface, but it can 
also weigh you down. As you descend into the depths, youâ€™ll encounter 
various hazards within the environment that will attempt to impede your 
progress. Only through strategic planning, efficient terrain navigation, 
and overcoming dangers would you complete the journey to the core of the 
earth. 

=========================================================================

Select [How to play] in the menu for a quick run down of what to do in game
Mine your way down to the core to win!
Beware of environmental hazards along the way...the world responds to your
actions.
Make use of the shop to sell resources you've mined for upgrades.

To move around, the default keybinds are:
* [A] 	- for moving left.
* [D] 	- for moving right
* [SPACE]	- for jumping, or Jetpack usage.

To access your inventory, press [I].

To access the shop, press [TAB]...but beware, it's only accessible on the
surface!

To mine, simply press or hold [LEFT CLICK] on your mouse. The elements
that you can mine will be highlighted in red.

=========================================================================

Special Controls: Press [LCTRL + 1] to open Debug Menu.
In Debug Menu:	  
* Click [Fog] to toggle Fog of War on/off. 
* Click [Mining Size] to toggle Mining size between 50 and 5.
* Click [Mining Range] to toggle between increased mining range and default mining range.
* Click [Capacity] to toggle max capacity between 999999999 and 2500.
* Click [Money] to gain 99999 gold.
* Click [Health] to toggle infinite health on/off.
* Click [Info] to toggle debug info (FPS counter, render mode, etc...) on/off.
* Click [Regen World] to regenerate a new world.
* Click [Reload Configs] to reload world gen configs.
* Click [Unlock Cam] to unlock the camera (stop following player, moveable with arrow keys).
* Click [Rotate Cam Left] to rotate camera counter-clockwise by 45 degrees.
* Click [Rotate Cam Right] to rotate camera clockwise by 45 degrees.
* Click [Reset Cam] to reset camera position and orientation (set camera position back to player).
* Click [Render Quad / Render Tex] to toggle between quad and tex render mode.
* Click [Multithread Off / Multithread On] to toggle multithreading on/off.
* Click [Show Grid] to toggle multithreading grid on/off.
* Click [Shop On / Shop Off] to toggle shop access at any level on/off.

Benchmark how to use:
Set how long to benchmark in "Assets\Configs\benchmarkConfig.txt". In Main menu, click "Benchmark" to start benchmarking. Once benchmark is done, game will automatically return to main menu. Results can be viewed from "BenchmarkResults.txt" in root exec folder

Minimum Specs: 
- i7-7700HQ or equivalent 
- GTX 1050 or equivalent
- 8GB RAM

Do note, if the minimum specs is not met, the game might dip below 60fps. 
Do not run game below 60 fps.
